6 - Contacts

6.1 - Listing Contacts

          When you first enter the contacts section, you will be presented with a side menu which allows you to display a paged list of all contacts, or only contacts whose name begins with a given letter of the alphabet. The resulting list will show the contact name, the company name, the contact's work number, and the contact's e-mail address. Clicking on the contact name will bring up a view of the contact. Clicking on the e-mail address will allow you to send an e-mail message to that contact. An additional option box beside the e-mail address serves to allow e-mail to be sent to multiple recipients, as well as choosing whether the e-mail is addressed directly to, carbon copied to, or blind carbon copied to the selected contact. Clicking on the 'Send E-Mail' link at the bottom of the list will open your default e-mail client with the selected addressed supplied in the address fields.

6.2 - Viewing and Editing Contacts

          The contact viewer can be accessed by several means, three of which are from calendar event views and orders for a contact, and of course, the contact search/list. Both the viewer and the editor display the same information, however the editor can be used to add, edit and delete contacts.

          Each contact view also has three links below the table which can be easily used to find all calendar events, calls, or orders associated with this contact.

    Username

          The 'Username' field is the login name of the contact which the contact can use to log in to a client access system (if one is made available).

    Password

          The 'Password' field is the password of the contact which the contact can use to log in to a client access system (if one is made available).

    Allow Login

          The 'Allow Login' field allows the contacts's ability to log into a client system to be easily enabled or disabled.

    Geographic Zone

          The 'Geographic Zone' field allows the contact to be associated with a defined area. This is primarily used as a tool to allow the 'event autoassign' feature to assign events to users who are in the same zone as the contact.

    Time Zone

          The 'Time Zone' field should be used by a client access system to determine which time zone a contact is in. This allows for proper adjustment of times to the contact's time zone.

    Surname

          The 'Surname' field is the surname or last name of the contact.

    Given Name

          The 'Given Name' field is the given name(s) of the contact.

    Salutation

          The 'Salutation' field is used to indicate the formal salutation of the contact, i.e. Mr, Mrs, Dr.

    Contact Type

          The 'Contact Type' field is used to indicate the general type of contact. This can be set to anything, but short descriptions such as 'Associate', 'Sales contact', 'Legal contact', etc. may be more useful.

    Referred By

          The 'Referred By' field is generally used to record the name of the person or company through whom this contact was first met.

    Alternate Contact

          The 'Alternate Contact' field is used to record the name of an alternate contact (i.e. next of kin).

    Billing Method

          The 'Billing Method' field is used to record the preferred billing method of the contact, assuming the contact is also a customer.

    Organization

          The 'Organization' field is used to record the company for which the contact works.

    Job Title

          The 'Job Title' field is used to indicate the contact's title at their place of work.

    Work Phone

          The 'Work Phone' field is used to record the work phone number (and extension) of the contact.

    Home Phone

          The 'Home Phone' field is used to record the home phone number of the contact.

    FAX Number

          The 'FAX Number' field is used to record the FAX number of the contact.

    Mobile Number

          The 'Mobile Number' field is used to record the cell phone or pager number of the contact.

    E-mail

          The 'E-mail' field is used to record the e-mail address of the contact. In view mode, clicking on the e-mail address will allow you to send e-mail to this contact.

    Home Address

          The 'Home Address' field is the street address of the contact's home address.

    Home City

          The 'Home City' field is the city of the contact's home address.

    Home Province

          The 'Home Province' field is the province (or state) of the contact's home address.

    Home Country

          The 'Home Country' field is the country of the contact's home address.

    Home Postal Code

          The 'Home Postal Code' field is the postal code (or zip code) of the contact's home address.

    Work Address

          The 'Work Address' field is the street address of the contact's work address.

    Work City

          The 'Work City' field is the city of the contact's work address.

    Work Province

          The 'Work Province' field is the province (or state) of the contact's work address.

    Work Country

          The 'Work Country' field is the country of the contact's work address.

    Work Postal Code

          The 'Work Postal Code' field is the postal code (or zip code) of the contact's work address.
