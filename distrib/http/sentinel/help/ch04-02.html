Chapter 4. Contacts and Companies

Viewing and Editing Contacts

      The contact viewer can be accessed by several means, three of which are from calendar event views and orders for a contact, and of course, the contact search/list. Both the viewer and the editor display the same information, however the editor can be used to add, edit and delete contacts.

      Each contact view also has two links below the table which can be easily used to find all calendar events or all orders that are associated with this contact.

Last Name

      The 'Last Name' field is the surname or last name of the contact.

First Name

      The 'First Name' field is the given name(s) of the contact.

Title

      The 'Title' field is used to indicate the job title of the user.

Company

      The 'Company' field is used to associate the contact with a company in the companies table. In view mode, clicking on the company name will take you directly to a view of the company.

Contact Type

      The 'Contact Type' field is used to indicate the general type of contact. This can be set to anything, but short descriptions such as 'Associate', 'Sales contact', 'Legal contact', etc. may be more useful.

Address

      The 'Address' field is the home street address of the contact.

City, Province

      The 'City, Province' field is the city and province (or state) in which the contact lives.

Country

      The 'Country ' field is the country in which the contact lives.

Postal Code

      The 'Postal Code' field is the postal code (or zip code) of the contact's home address.

Work Phone

      The 'Work Phone' field is used to record the work phone number (and extension) of the contact.

Home Phone

      The 'Home Phone' field is used to record the home phone number of the contact.

FAX Number

      The 'FAX Number' field is used to record the FAX number of the contact.

Cellular

      The 'Cellular' field is used to record the cell phone number of the contact.

Pager

      The 'Pager' field is used to record the pager number of the contact.

E-mail

      The 'E-mail' field is used to record the e-mail address of the contact. In view mode, clicking on the e-mail address will allow you to send e-mail to this contact.

Last Meeting

      The 'Last Meeting' field is used to record the last meeting date with the contact.

Referred By

      The 'Referred By' field is generally used to record a name of the person or company through whom this contact was first met.

Customer

      The 'Customer' field is used to indicate whether or not this contact is also a customer.

Notes

      The 'Notes' field is generally used to record any notes or reminders for a contact which do not fit into any of the other fields.
