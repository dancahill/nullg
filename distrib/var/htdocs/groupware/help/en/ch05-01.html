Chapter 5. Calls

Listing Calls

      When you first enter the calls section, you will be presented with a list of all calls, with the newest calls appearing at the top of the list. If the number of accessible calls exceeds the user's maximum list length, then this list will be broken up into multiple pages. This list will show the call id, the call action type, the contact associated with this call (if any), and the date and time the call occurred. Clicking on the call id will bring up a view of that call. Clicking on the contact name will bring up a view of the associated contact.
